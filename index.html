About me

Lucas was born and raised in Montauk, NY. He went to Northeastern University, earning a BS in Business Administration. For the past two years, Lucas worked on the sales/partnerships team at Andela, helping growing tech companies extend their engineering teams with software developers from across Africa.

Recently, Lucas moved to Austin, Texas. There, he realized his passion for software engineering. He is currently in a full-stack JavaScript bootcamp at The University of Texas, Austin. In the future, Lucas has aspirations to join a development team at a growing company making a positive impact on the world.

In his free time, Lucas enjoys cooking, reading and hiking. He also plays Guitar and Drums and is a former member of the NYC band, CRV.
